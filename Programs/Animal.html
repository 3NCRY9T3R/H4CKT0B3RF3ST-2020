Rabbit

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidae of the order Lagomorpha (along with the hare and the pika).

  • Kingdom:Animalia
  • Phylum:Chordata
  • Subphylum:Vertebrata
  • Class:Mammalia
  • Order:Lagomorpha
  • Family:Leporidae

The European rabbit,which has been introduced on every continent except Antartica,is familiar throughout the world as a wild prey animal and as a domesticated from livestock and pet.A female Rabbit is called a doe and the male one is called a buck.
